Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe on how to make a really tasty lasagna

It's quick and easy to make. And so tasty

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meal sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna noodles -> sauce -> noodles
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
